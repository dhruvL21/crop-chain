# **App Name**: AgriNexus

## Core Features:

- Farmer Shop: E-commerce platform for purchasing farming supplies, including seeds, tools, and equipment.
- Farmer Marketplace: Direct farmer-to-buyer marketplace for selling crops with wholesale and retail pricing.
- Smart Dashboard: Personalized dashboard for sales, revenue, price trends, and inventory insights.
- AI Assistant: AI chatbot to answer farming questions and guide users through buying/selling processes.
- Sales Optimization Tool: AI engine that analyzes market demand and supply to recommend pricing strategies and predict trends.
- Secure Authentication: User authentication with role-based access control.
- Multi-Language Support: Support for regional languages and English to cater to a diverse user base.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23) to represent agriculture and nature. The concept is to convey a sense of growth, health, and connection to the land. Choosing green will resonate with the farming community and reinforce the app's focus.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a very desaturated version of the primary hue.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700) for highlighting important information and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a balance of modern look and warmth. 
- Use clear, modern icons representing crops, equipment, analytics, and other key features.
- Mobile-first design with a clean, intuitive layout optimized for farmers and rural users.
- Subtle animations for data updates and AI assistant interactions to enhance user experience.
